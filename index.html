Our Services

What We Do Best

We offer a full range of residential and commercial renovation services.

Framing

Structural Framework

Strong, precise framing is the backbone of every renovation. We build solid structural frameworks for walls, ceilings, and additions, ensuring your project starts on a sturdy, level foundation built to code.

  • Wall framing & partitions
  • Ceiling & roof framing
  • Structural supports
  • Additions & extensions

Drywall & Taping

Smooth Professional Finishes

From installation to finishing, we deliver seamless drywall work with expert taping, mudding, and sanding. Every surface is left smooth, even, and ready for paint or texture — no imperfections, no shortcuts.

  • Drywall installation
  • Taping & mudding
  • Sanding & finishing
  • Repairs & patching

Painting

Interior & Exterior

A fresh coat of paint transforms any space. We provide clean, professional painting for walls, ceilings, trim, and exteriors using premium paints and meticulous prep work for a flawless, long-lasting finish.

  • Interior wall & ceiling painting
  • Exterior house painting
  • Trim & cabinet painting
  • Surface prep & priming

Flooring Installation

All Types

We install all types of flooring — hardwood, laminate, vinyl, and engineered — with precision cuts and a perfect fit. Every floor is installed level and smooth for a beautiful, durable result throughout your home or business.

  • Hardwood & engineered wood
  • Laminate & vinyl plank
  • Subfloor preparation
  • Trim & transitions

Tile Installation

Floors, Walls & Backsplashes

From bathroom floors to kitchen backsplashes, our tile work is precise and built to last. We handle all tile types — ceramic, porcelain, marble, and mosaic — with clean lines and expertly sealed grout.

  • Floor & wall tile
  • Kitchen backsplashes
  • Shower & bathroom tile
  • Grouting & sealing

General Remodeling

Full-Service Renovation

From concept to completion, we manage your entire renovation project. Kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and full interior finishing — we coordinate every detail so you get a polished result on time and on budget.

  • Kitchen & bathroom remodeling
  • Basement finishing
  • Interior finishing
  • Commercial renovations
